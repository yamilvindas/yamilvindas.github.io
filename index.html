Short Bio

I am a post-doctoral fellow at the Centre of Innovation in Telecommunications and Integration of service (CITI) in Lyon, France. My current research focuses on deep learning methods applied to telecommunications, with particular interest in self-supervised learning online techniques for wireless channel charting.

Research Interests

Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Self-supervised learning, Semi-supervised learning, Artificial Intelligence, Model Compression, Medical Imaging, Telecommunications, Medical Imaging.
